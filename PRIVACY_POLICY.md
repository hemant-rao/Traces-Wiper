# Privacy Policy for Traces Wiper

**Effective Date:** June 5, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Traces Wiper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the highest level of security for your data. This Privacy Policy outlines how Traces Wiper handles your data, permissions, and internal processes. 

Traces Wiper is designed as a **100% offline, client-side application**. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal data or files with any external servers or third parties.

## 2. Permissions Required

To function properly, Traces Wiper requires specific local device permissions:

*   **Read & Write Storage Permissions (Files and Media):** 
    *   **Purpose:** The app requires access to your local file system to identify leftover traces of deleted files, cached data, and orphans, as well as to securely overwrite and destroy files you explicitly choose to delete.
    *   **Usage:** Storage access is strictly limited to scanning for traces and executing local cryptographic overwriting algorithms. No file information or content is ever uploaded, transmitted, or logged outside of your device.

## 3. Secure Deletion and Data Handling

Traces Wiper utilizes advanced security algorithms (such as US DoD 5220.22-M, Gutmann, and Random Overwrite) to ensure that your deleted data is permanently destroyed.

*   **Local Overwriting:** When you initiate a wipe, the app overrides the physical storage blocks containing your data with randomized data patterns. This process happens entirely on your local device hardware.
*   **Irreversibility:** The core function of this app is to make data unrecoverable. Once data is securely wiped using Traces Wiper, we cannot restore it. 
*   **No Data Transmission:** We do not track what files you shred, what traces you clean, or when you use the app. All logs relating to shredding history are stored locally on your device within a secured local database and can be cleared by you at any time.

## 4. Third-Party Services and Analytics

Traces Wiper **does not** integrate with any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising networks. Moreso, the app does not request or require network permissions, rendering it physically incapable of transmitting data over the internet.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer team through the Play Store listing.
